About Me

I am a PhD student in Electronic Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship, Belt and Road Scholarship, and the CUHK Vice Chancellor's Scholarship. My research interests lie in medical robotics, AI-driven medical instrumentation, and soft/continuum robotic systems.

I previously completed my BEng in Mechanical & Automation Engineering at CUHK, graduating Rank 1 in my cohort with a major GPA of 3.9/4.0. I have experience in robotics, flexible sensors, laser-based biomedical instrumentation, microcontroller systems, and medical device prototyping.
